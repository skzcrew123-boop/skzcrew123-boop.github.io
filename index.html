About the Game

Fraco Charmer is a unique and stylish adventure game featuring interesting mechanics, funny NPC interactions, Easter eggs, and challenging levels. Explore multiple levels like "Level 1 - The Weak" and meet quirky characters who react to your actions. Unlock badges by finding hidden secrets and enjoy the humor scattered throughout the game world.
